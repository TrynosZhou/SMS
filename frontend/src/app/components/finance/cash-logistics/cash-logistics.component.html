Logistics receipts

Total Transport Receipts and Total DH Receipts: if a payment is at or below the student’s transport or DH line on the invoice, the whole payment counts toward that fee (e.g. a full $90 DH payment). Larger payments are split across tuition, transport, and DH in proportion to those invoice lines. Transport: day scholars who use school transport. DH: day scholars on dining hall. Date filters apply to payment dates.

How to read these numbers

  • Large figure on each card (e.g. $15,840 / $28,080) is this term’s billed transport or DH for the eligible cohort — it is not cash collected.
  • Total Transport Receipts / Total DH Receipts sum how much of each payment is assigned to that fee (payments ≤ the fee line go entirely to that fee; bigger payments are split by invoice proportions). They are not “eligible students × fee.”
  • The table lists {{ paymentLinesInScope }} line(s) with a positive transport share (day scholars who use transport; $0 hidden). That matches the {{ cashLineCountTransport }} line(s) in Total Transport Receipts.
  • The table lists {{ paymentLinesInScope }} line(s) with a positive DH share (day scholars on dining hall; boarders excluded; $0 hidden). That matches the {{ cashLineCountDH }} line(s) in Total DH Receipts.
